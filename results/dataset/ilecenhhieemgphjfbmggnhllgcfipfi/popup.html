What is the extension key and where can I find it?

Extension key is a one time unique code that was used to authenticate plugin for the first time. Your account will be automatically validated and added to the plugin using this key.


You will get the key from the lync launcher. your institution should have communicated with you regarding accessing the launcher.


If you need help, contact your institution administration or IT department or send an email to help@letslync.com with your details (name,any school id or email, institution name, location).

What is Organization ID and how can I get this?

Organization ID is a unique code that was used to identify your institution.


You will get the Organization ID from the your institution. Your institution should have communicated with you regarding accessing this plugin.


If you need help, contact your institution administration or IT department or send an email to help@letslync.com with your details (name,any school id or email, institution name, location).
